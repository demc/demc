Strengths Profile

Future Thinker

I spend a lot of time thinking about the future. Often I am so preoccupied with the future that its as though I am not present in the moment. Imagining many possile futures for myself fuels my motivation to work hard today to have a better tomorrow.

I deploy this strength every day as an educator. Designing lessons and projects requires substantial time dedicated toward thinking about the future. Our curriculum originates from a vision I conceive about what skills and knowledge I hope students will obtain.

I am filled with energy, hope, and determination when I am contributing to a significant cause or idea. Likewise, I have found myself feeling despondent when the my work is frivolous in nature. My affinity for educating youth comes from my desire to contribute something significant.

How I can use this strength:
  • Planning and creating a vision.
  • Building things that matter.
  • Helping people believe in an idea.
